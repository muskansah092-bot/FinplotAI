GENERAL FINANCIAL TIPS

1. Build an emergency fund covering at least 6 months of essential expenses before focusing heavily on other financial goals.

2. Keep your total EMI/loan payments below 40% of your monthly income to avoid debt stress.

3. Automate savings and investments right after salary credit, so saving happens before spending, not after.

4. Review your financial health at least once every 3 months, not just once a year.

5. Prioritize high-interest debt repayment before investing in lower-return instruments.

6. Maintain separate funds for emergencies, short-term goals, and long-term goals — do not mix them.

7. Increase your savings rate gradually with every salary increment instead of increasing lifestyle spending proportionally.

8. Always have adequate health and term life insurance before focusing on wealth creation, especially if you have dependents.
