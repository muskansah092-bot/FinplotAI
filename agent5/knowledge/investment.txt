INVESTMENT GUIDELINES

1. Match investment choices to risk profile:
   - Conservative: Fixed Deposits, PPF, Debt Mutual Funds
   - Moderate: Balanced/Hybrid Funds, Index Funds, some Gold
   - Aggressive: Equity Mutual Funds, Direct Stocks, Small-cap exposure

2. Diversify across asset classes (equity, debt, gold) rather than concentrating in one type of investment.

3. For goals more than 5 years away, equity-heavy allocations are generally suitable due to long-term compounding.

4. For goals under 2 years away, prioritize capital protection over high returns — avoid equity for short-term goals.

5. Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs) reduce the impact of market timing and are suitable for salaried individuals investing monthly.

6. Rebalance investment portfolios periodically (e.g., once a year) to maintain the intended risk allocation as markets shift.

7. Tax-saving instruments like PPF, ELSS, and NPS should be considered as part of the overall investment plan, not as an afterthought.
